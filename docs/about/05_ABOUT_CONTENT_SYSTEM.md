---

# Introduction

This document defines what the About section should communicate.

Not the final copywriting.

Not marketing text.

It defines the storytelling structure.

Every sentence should strengthen trust.

---

# Core Objective

The visitor should finish this section thinking:

"I understand why UTBEX exists."

Not

"I know everything about UTBEX."

Leave room for curiosity.

---

# Storytelling Formula

Reality

↓

Opportunity

↓

Action

↓

Impact

↓

Future

Never change this order.

---

# Communication Style

Warm.

Calm.

Professional.

Human.

Honest.

Confident.

Minimal.

Never corporate.

Never exaggerated.

Never emotional manipulation.

---

# Content Hierarchy

Section Label

↓

Headline

↓

Supporting Story

↓

Quote

↓

Small CTA

Every block has one responsibility.

---

# Section Label

Very short.

Examples

ABOUT

OUR PURPOSE

WHY WE EXIST

Never use long labels.

---

# Headline

Maximum three lines.

Emotion first.

Information second.

Examples of direction

Building Better Futures Together.

Creating Opportunities That Matter.

Growing Communities Through Innovation.

Do not use buzzwords.

Do not oversell.

---

# Supporting Story

Maximum three short paragraphs.

Simple language.

Natural reading rhythm.

Every paragraph should answer one question.

---

# Quote

Only one quote.

Maximum two lines.

Simple.

Honest.

Human.

Preferred source:

Founder

Community

Partner

Government

Never use motivational quotes.

---

# CTA

Soft CTA.

Never sales oriented.

Examples

Discover Our Journey

See Our Projects

Continue Exploring

Learn More

---

# Writing Rules

Short sentences.

Simple vocabulary.

Natural rhythm.

No repetition.

No filler words.

No marketing clichés.

Every sentence must earn its place.

---

# Success Checklist

✓ Easy to read.

---

# Final Principle

The About section should sound like a conversation.

Not an advertisement.

Not a presentation.

Not a brochure.

If visitors feel they have discovered UTBEX instead of being introduced to it,

the content has succeeded.
