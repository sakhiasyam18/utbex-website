# Introduction

The About section is no longer a company profile.

It is a visual documentary.

The visitor should feel like opening the second chapter of a documentary film.

Hero introduces the organization.

About introduces the people.

---

# Emotional Direction

Hero creates curiosity.

About creates empathy.

Portfolio creates confidence.

Contact creates action.

Never break this emotional progression.

---

# Documentary Philosophy

Never create a gallery.

Never create a collage just because it looks beautiful.

Every image must tell part of the story.

Every photograph is evidence.

# Photography Rules

Preferred

Real workshops

Real mentoring

Real villages

Real UMKM

Real discussions

Real government collaboration

Real presentations

Real production process

Real smiles

Real documentation

Avoid

Office desks

People looking at camera

Handshake photos

Award ceremonies

Empty meeting rooms

Stock photography

AI generated humans

Corporate portraits

---

# Composition Philosophy

Do not build a grid.

Do not build Pinterest.

Do not build Masonry.

Instead,

compose the photographs like pages inside a documentary magazine.

Every image has a reason.

Every image has hierarchy.

# Editorial Composition

The composition should never feel mathematically perfect.

Slight asymmetry creates authenticity.

Slight overlap creates depth.

Large whitespace creates elegance.

---

# Documentary Evidence

Instead of floating marketing cards,

use documentary annotations.

Example

2024

Malang

or

These feel like documentation,

---

# Caption Philosophy

Small.

Quiet.

Professional.

Placed close to photographs.

Never loud.

Never colorful.

Never marketing.

---

# Quote Placement

Never isolate the quote.

The quote should feel connected to the story.

It belongs beside the documentary.

Not inside a separate card.

---

# Reading Rhythm

Photo

↓

Read

↓

Observe

↓

Photo

↓

Read

↓

Reflect

↓

Continue

Scrolling should feel slower than Hero.

---

# White Space

Increase whitespace by approximately 30%.

Visitors should breathe.

The layout should never feel crowded.

---

# Color

Almost monochrome.

Brand color appears only when necessary.

Photography carries the emotion.

Color supports hierarchy.

---

# Depth

Use soft layering.

Very soft shadows.

Very subtle glass.

Almost invisible.

Nothing dramatic.

---

# Motion

Images appear naturally.

Like someone placing documentary photos on a table.

Never fly in.

Never bounce.

Never rotate.

---

# Scroll Feeling

Hero

↓

Calm

↓

Documentary

↓

Editorial

↓

Portfolio

The transition should feel invisible.

---

# Success Metrics

Visitors should think

"This organization genuinely works with communities."

instead of

"This organization has a beautiful website."

If the photographs become more memorable than the UI,

the About section has succeeded.

---

# Final Principle

The About section should not look designed.

It should look discovered.

Like opening the notebook of someone who has spent years working with communities.

That feeling is more valuable than any animation, gradient, or visual effect.
