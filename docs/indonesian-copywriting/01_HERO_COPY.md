# UTBEX DIGITAL EXPERIENCE SYSTEM

## HERO COPYWRITING

Version: 1.0

Status: LOCKED

Language: English (Documentation)
Output: Indonesian Website Copy

Audience:

Creative Director

Copywriter

Content Strategist

Frontend Engineer

AI Coding Assistant

---

# Introduction

The Hero is the first conversation between UTBEX and its visitors.

It should create curiosity,

build trust,

and communicate purpose within the first few seconds.

The Hero should never attempt to explain everything.

Instead,

it should invite visitors to continue the journey.

---

# Communication Objective

The Hero must answer one question:

"Why should I continue scrolling?"

It should not describe every service.

It should create confidence.

---

# Emotional Goal

Visitors should immediately feel:

• Human

• Hopeful

• Professional

• Authentic

• Modern

• Calm

Avoid creating excitement through exaggerated marketing language.

Trust grows through clarity.

---

# Tone of Voice

The Hero speaks like an experienced mentor.

Not a salesperson.

Not a government institution.

Not a startup trying to impress.

The language should feel confident,

warm,

and humble.

---

# Headline Principles

A headline should:

• Be short.

• Be memorable.

• Focus on impact.

• Use everyday Indonesian.

• Avoid jargon.

Maximum:

Two lines.

---

# Avoid Headlines Like

"Kami adalah perusahaan yang berkomitmen memberikan solusi terbaik."

"Transformasi Digital Berbasis Inovasi."

"Memberdayakan Masyarakat Melalui Teknologi."

These feel generic.

---

# Preferred Headlines

Mengubah Ide Menjadi Dampak Nyata.

Bersama Membangun Masa Depan.

Kolaborasi yang Menghasilkan Perubahan.

Inovasi Dimulai dari Masyarakat.

Perubahan Selalu Dimulai dari Langkah Pertama.

---

# Recommended Primary Headline

Mengubah Ide Menjadi Dampak Nyata.

Reason:

Simple.

Strong.

Easy to remember.

Represents the mission of UTBEX.

---

# Subheadline Principles

The subheadline explains the headline.

It should answer:

How?

Who?

Why?

Maximum:

Three short sentences.

Avoid corporate language.

---

# Recommended Subheadline

Bersama desa, UMKM, dan komunitas, kami menghadirkan inovasi yang tumbuh dari kebutuhan nyata. Setiap program dirancang untuk menciptakan perubahan yang dapat dirasakan dan terus berkembang bersama masyarakat.

---

# CTA Principles

CTA should feel like an invitation.

Never like a sales button.

Preferred verbs:

Lihat

Jelajahi

Mulai

Temukan

Pelajari

Bangun

Avoid:

Daftar Sekarang

Beli Sekarang

Hubungi Kami Sekarang

---

# Primary CTA

Lihat Perjalanan Kami

---

# Secondary CTA

Mulai Kolaborasi

---

# Supporting Statement

A short supporting sentence may appear below the CTA.

Example:

Dipercaya berbagai desa, UMKM, komunitas, dan institusi untuk menciptakan dampak yang berkelanjutan.

---

# Relationship With Photography

The Hero copy should never compete with photography.

Photography attracts emotion.

Copy provides meaning.

Maintain visual balance.

---

# Relationship With Animation

Animations should support reading.

Never distract from the headline.

Visitors should finish reading comfortably before animations draw attention elsewhere.

---

# Responsive Behaviour

On mobile:

Do not rewrite the copy.

Adjust layout only.

Maintain the same emotional message across every device.

---

# Accessibility

Headlines must remain semantic.

Use proper heading hierarchy.

Ensure CTA labels remain descriptive.

---

# Validation Checklist

✓ Human.

✓ Short.

✓ Clear.

✓ Memorable.

✓ Professional.

✓ Authentic.

✓ Indonesian-first.

✓ Easy to scan.

---

# Final Principle

If visitors remember only one sentence after leaving the website,

it should be:

"**Mengubah Ide Menjadi Dampak Nyata.**"

The Hero has successfully introduced the identity of UTBEX.
