---

# Introduction

The Footer should never feel separated from the Portfolio.

Visitors should not notice where one section ends and another begins.

The atmosphere should slowly become quieter.

---

# Visual Goal

Create the feeling of reading the final page of a premium documentary.

Everything becomes calmer.

Everything breathes.

Nothing asks for attention.

---

# Visual DNA

Hero

↓

Premium

About

↓

Editorial

Portfolio

↓

Documentary

Footer

↓

Reflection

The Footer is the quietest section of the website.

---

# Composition

One Closing Statement.

↓

Essential Navigation.

↓

Contact.

↓

Social.

↓

Copyright.

Nothing else.

---

# Visual Weight

The closing statement receives the highest attention.

Navigation becomes secondary.

Everything below becomes progressively quieter.

---

# Background

Continue the white foundation.

Very soft warm gradient.

Almost invisible.

No obvious color transition.

The background should feel continuous with Portfolio.

---

# Typography

Large editorial headline.

Comfortable paragraph width.

Small navigation labels.

Quiet legal text.

No bold visual hierarchy after the closing statement.

---

# Color Philosophy

White remains dominant.

Dark typography.

Accent color appears only for interaction.

Never use multiple accent colors.

---

# Divider

Avoid heavy horizontal lines.

If separation is needed,

use whitespace first.

Only use an extremely subtle divider if necessary.

---

# Social Icons

Simple outline style.

Uniform size.

Soft hover.

No colorful brand icons.

No circular backgrounds.

They should feel integrated into the editorial design.

---

# Navigation

Limit navigation to essential destinations.

Avoid long lists.

Visitors should immediately understand where to go next.

---

# Whitespace

Generous top spacing.

Comfortable internal spacing.

Compact ending.

The Footer should never feel compressed.

---

# Responsive Strategy

Desktop

Editorial alignment.

Tablet

Balanced spacing.

Mobile

Centered reading flow.

Large touch targets.

Simple hierarchy.

---

# Visual Checklist

✓ Calm.

✓ Bright.

✓ Editorial.

✓ Premium.

✓ Minimal.

✓ Consistent with Portfolio.

✓ Comfortable reading rhythm.

---

# Final Principle

The Footer should quietly disappear into the end of the page.

Visitors should remember the feeling,

not the layout.
