CRITICAL

Audience:

---

# Objective

Validate the complete website before production.

Every feature, interaction, animation, layout, and page should be tested.

Nothing reaches production without passing QA.

---

# QA Philosophy

Quality is not tested at the end.

Quality is verified before release.

---

# Functional Testing

Verify:

- Navigation works.
- All buttons work.
- CTA works.
- Links work.
- Forms work.
- Scroll works.
- Mobile menu works.

---

No broken layouts.
