Version: 1.0

Status: LOCKED

Language: English

Audience:

Creative Director

UI Designer

Motion Designer

Frontend Engineer

AI Coding Assistant

---

# Introduction

Light is the emotional material of the UTBEX experience.

Before visitors recognize typography,

before they appreciate spacing,

before they notice photography,

they unconsciously perceive light.

Ambient light defines the emotional climate of the website.

---

# Philosophy

Light should never become an effect.

Light should become atmosphere.

Visitors should feel warmth,

depth,

and calmness,

without ever identifying where the light comes from.

---

# Emotional Purpose

Ambient lighting exists to:

Create warmth.

Increase depth.

Connect sections.

Reduce visual flatness.

Guide emotional transitions.

It never exists to attract attention.

---

# Lighting Hierarchy

Global Ambient Light

↓

Section Ambient Light

↓

Component Reflection

↓

Micro Highlights

Every layer becomes progressively weaker.

---

# Global Ambient

The entire website should share one invisible light environment.

Think of sunlight entering a quiet architectural studio.

Soft.

Wide.

Natural.

Consistent.

---

# Section Lighting

Each chapter receives its own emotional atmosphere.

Hero

Warm optimism.

↓

About

Soft clarity.

↓

Portfolio

Balanced neutrality.

↓

Impact

Slight optimism.

↓

Footer

Gentle fade into silence.

The transitions between them should remain invisible.

---

# Radial Light System

Primary ambient lights should always be radial.

Characteristics:

Very large.

Very soft.

Very blurred.

Low opacity.

Never create visible circles.

Never reveal the light source.

---

# Color Temperature

Primary

Warm White

Secondary

Ivory

Supporting

Neutral White

Accent

Extremely soft UTBEX Maroon

Avoid cold blue lighting.

Avoid saturated colors.

---

# Light Positioning

Preferred positions:

Top Left

Top Center

Upper Right

Behind Hero

Behind Glass Components

Behind Editorial Headlines

Never place obvious light sources directly behind body text.

---

# Diffusion

Every light should naturally dissolve into the canvas.

No hard edges.

No obvious gradients.

Only soft atmospheric transitions.

---

# Glass Interaction

Glass surfaces should receive ambient light naturally.

Allow:

soft reflections

edge illumination

internal diffusion

subtle brightness variation

Never simulate polished chrome.

---

# Motion

Ambient lighting may breathe.

Allowed:

Extremely slow opacity changes.

Very slow diffusion movement.

Duration should exceed several seconds.

The movement should remain almost impossible to detect.

---

# Lighting Density

Hero

Highest atmosphere.

↓

About

Slightly calmer.

↓

Portfolio

Balanced.

↓

Impact

Subtle optimism.

↓

Footer

Gradually disappearing.

Never let every section have the same intensity.

---

# Layer Relationship

Ambient lighting always exists behind content.

Never above typography.

Never above photography.

Never reduce readability.

---

# Performance

Prefer CSS radial gradients.

Prefer blur filters.

Avoid video backgrounds.

Avoid animated light assets.

Keep rendering lightweight.

---

# Responsive Behaviour

Ambient light scales naturally.

Do not remove it on mobile.

Simply reduce its physical size while preserving the same emotional feeling.

---

# Lighting Checklist

✓ Invisible.

✓ Warm.

✓ Layered.

✓ Soft.

✓ Editorial.

✓ Premium.

✓ Lightweight.

✓ Consistent.

---

# Final Principle

Visitors should never notice the lighting itself.

They should only feel that every section naturally belongs to the same beautifully illuminated world.
