# UTBEX DIGITAL EXPERIENCE SYSTEM

## PORTFOLIO CONTENT SYSTEM

Version: 1.0

Status: LOCKED

Language: English

Audience:
Creative Director
Content Writer
Frontend Engineer
AI Coding Assistant

---

# Introduction

The Portfolio does not explain projects.

It tells transformation stories.

Every sentence exists to provide context for the documentary.

Nothing more.

Nothing less.

---

# Core Objective

After reading a project,

the visitor should understand

why it mattered,

who it helped,

and what changed.

Not what technology was used.

---

# Writing Philosophy

Write like a documentary narrator.

Not like a marketing agency.

Not like a company profile.

Not like an annual report.

Simple.

Honest.

Human.

---

# Story Formula

Context

↓

Challenge

↓

Collaboration

↓

Execution

↓

Transformation

↓

Future

Every project follows this structure.

---

# Headline

Maximum two lines.

Focus on people.

Not deliverables.

Good

Helping Local Coffee Farmers Reach Digital Markets

Avoid

Coffee Branding Project

---

# Intro Paragraph

One short paragraph.

Set the context.

Explain why this project exists.

Never explain everything.

---

# Challenge

Describe the real challenge.

Use simple language.

Avoid technical jargon.

Make the visitor care.

---

# Collaboration

Explain how UTBEX worked together.

Never use

"We helped..."

Instead,

show collaboration.

Examples

Working alongside...

Together with...

In partnership with...

---

# Execution

Describe the process briefly.

Workshop.

Discussion.

Field Visit.

Training.

Mentoring.

Implementation.

No unnecessary details.

---

# Transformation

This is the most important part.

Show the difference.

Before.

↓

After.

Visitors should clearly feel progress.

---

# Evidence

Evidence replaces marketing claims.

Examples

120 MSMEs participated.

Three villages involved.

Six months mentoring.

Official government partnership.

Evidence should always be factual.

---

# Quote

Maximum one quote.

From

Community.

Partner.

Student.

Government.

Never from UTBEX itself.

---

# CTA

Soft.

Examples

Read Full Story

Explore The Journey

Discover More

Never

Buy Now

Contact Sales

Start Today

---

# Writing Style

Short sentences.

Natural rhythm.

No buzzwords.

No exaggerated language.

No empty adjectives.

Every sentence earns its place.

---

# Reading Speed

Visitors should slow down.

Read.

Observe.

Continue.

The Portfolio should feel intentional.

Never rushed.

---

# Checklist

✓ Human.

✓ Honest.

✓ Documentary.

✓ Easy to read.

✓ Evidence driven.

✓ Community centered.

✓ Consistent with Hero and About.

---

# Final Principle

The content should disappear behind the story.

Visitors should remember the transformation,

not the writing.

If the people become more memorable than the paragraphs,

the content system has succeeded.

---

End of Document.
